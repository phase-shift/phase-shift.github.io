Phase Shift

Phase Shift is a project started by Andrei Jay and Paloma Kop that aims to build connections between artists and organizations in different geographic areas to form an inclusive, community-driven network.

With this as our goal, we are currently traveling across North America in a 1988 Chevrolet van, visiting artist spaces, organizing events, and spending time in nature.
