Friends of Phase Shift

  • Polyphase Portal - an online space for hosting workshops and classes, which we help run
  • Scanlines.xyz - a forum for diy a/v, which we also help run
  • Underscores Shop - an open video hardware label by our friend cyberboy666
  • Coaxial Arts - a non-profit, multi-disciplinary media arts organization in LA devoted to media, sound, and performance art
  • Signal Culture - an organization that encourages creation of new work, building of community, and connection to history in the field of experimental media art by providing artists, researchers, and innovators with residencies, resources, and exhibition opportunities, now based in Loveland, Colorado
  • Indexical - a space in Santa Cruz, CA that engages the public in radical and unfamiliar artistic work through an ongoing series of performances, workshops, and publications
  • FEED - a newly founded Media Art Center based in Erie PA that hosts artist residencies, exhibitions, and media arts events
  • Synth Library Portland - a collectively run arts organization that supports a diverse community of artists, centering access for marginalized folks to experiment, learn, and participate
  • Patchwerks - a synth boutique in Seattle that hosts classes and community events related to elecronic music and art
  • Wavefield - a video art educational space located in Brooklyn, NY
