PHASE SPACE ARCHIVE

Phase Space was a DIY makerspace, multimedia art studio, and experimental collective in Brooklyn, NY that was active from 2019-2023. In that time, we held many workshops, shows, and community events with a focus on analog and experimental video art.

Here are a few of the artists who helped organize Phase Space over the years:

  • Andrei Jay
  • Paloma Kop
  • Irfan Brković
  • Max Coker
  • cable_visions
  • Jonathan Sims

As of June 2023, Phase Space as we knew it is no longer active. Andrei and Paloma have left Brooklyn to travel around the US and continue making art and organizing workshops and events as Phase Shift, the nomadic continuation of Phase Space. They have also joined up with several others to found Polyphase Portal, an online space for hosting workshops and classes. And Jonathan and cable_visions remain in our old space with their new project, Wavefield.
